For the improvement suggestions section, focus on:
- Specific sections of the resume that need rewording or expansion
- Quantifiable achievements the candidate should add (e.g., "Add metrics to your project outcomes")
- Format and structure improvements that help ATS parsers
- Skills or certifications worth acquiring to strengthen the application
- Tailoring language to mirror the job description's terminology

Each suggestion should be immediately actionable and specific to the role, not generic advice.
